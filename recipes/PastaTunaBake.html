Tuna Pasta Bake

Description

A tuna pasta bake is a comforting casserole dish made from pasta, canned tuna, and a sauce, typically topped with cheese and baked until golden.

Ingredients

  • 600g Rigatoni
  • 50g butter
  • 50g plain flour
  • 600ml milk
  • 250g strong cheddar grated
  • 2x160g of tuna steak drained
  • handful of chopped parsley
    •

    Method

    1. Heat oven to 180C/fan 160C/gas 4.
    2. oil 600g rigatoni for 2 mins less time than stated on the pack.
    3. To make the sauce, melt 50g butter in a saucepan and stir in 50g plain flour.
    4. Cook for 1 min, then gradually stir in 600ml milk to make a thick white sauce.
    5. Remove from the heat and stir in all but a handful of the 250g grated cheddar.
    6. Drain the pasta, mix with the white sauce, two 160g drained cans tuna, and a large handful of chopped parsley, then season.
    7. Transfer to a baking dish and top with the rest of the grated cheddar.
    8. Bake for 15-20 mins until the cheese on top is golden and starting to brown.

    Macros

  • Calories: ~4,250 kcal
  • Protein: ~190 g
  • Carbohydrates: ~330 g
  • Fat: ~210 g
    •

    Tips

    Tubular pasta works best in bakes. Instead of rigatoni, you could use penne or macaroni instead.
